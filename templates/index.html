Welcome to The Resin Crafter's Helper!

Do you do resin crafts and like to take notes?


Maybe you want to recreate a project and can't remember exactly what you did
and you have not been able to recreate it yet?

As a resin crafter myself, I know how important it can be to take notes during an experimental project. I think we can all agree that resin is expensive, and resin crafting can be a costly hobby. So anything that we can do to save on cost can be worth its weight in gold. Less waste will definitely help with this.

I watch a lot of YouTubers who do resin crafts and one thing I noticed is that a lot of them do take notes and/or rewatch their videos. So I decided to start taking notes when I do my projects and it makes a world of difference when trying to recreate that perfect pour. However, the problem is keeping all those notes organized.

Then I started to search around to see if there was anything out there that was specific to resin crafters for keeping all their notes in one place. Much to my surprise, I could not find anything that was already available. Then I had to do a final project for my CS50 course at Harvard University and saw this as a perfect opportunity to create something to help us all.

This is how the idea of The Resin Crafter's Helper was born.

This great tool is accessible from any computer where you have access to the internet.

This web-based application is loaded with some great features and I plan on continuing to work on it to bring its user even more features.
Some of the current features already available are:
  • You can securely register for an account and log in
  • Add a new project and upload an image of the mold and results
  • Display all the details of just a single project
  • View the details of all projects currently saved in your account
  • Edit any or all details for a single project
  • Delete a single project (this can not be undone)
  • Edit your account details and change your password
